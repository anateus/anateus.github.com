September 15, 2010

Future Jewish post-human minds are going to have to turn to their post-human Rabbis and ask:
<ul><li>Is running on Shabbes considered a violation? Or does that count under Pikuach Nefesh?</li> <li>How do you circumcise? Drop the least significant bit?

</li></ul>Can you think of others? Better clear these things now, before all uploadees count as Apikoros :>
