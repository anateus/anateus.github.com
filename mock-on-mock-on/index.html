Mock on, mock on...

January 22, 2007

Though I do adore William Blake, in a certain poem of his he sets all of modern science and philosphy to be but a mockery at religion’s expense and like sand, they are thrown back at the Mocking Eye.

These days, that same Mocking Eye is perhaps a better thing to be.

Mock on, mock on... - January 22, 2007 - Michael Katsevman
